## 3. Prohibited Activities

You must not use the Service to:

### 3.1 Illegal Activities

- Violate any applicable law, regulation, or legal requirement
- Facilitate or promote illegal activities
- Process data without a lawful basis under data protection laws
- Discriminate unlawfully against tenants or applicants
- Breach housing, tenancy, or landlord-tenant laws
- Evade Right to Rent or other immigration checks
- Engage in money laundering or financial crimes
- Infringe intellectual property rights of others

### 3.2 Harmful Content

- Upload, transmit, or store any content that is:
  - Defamatory, libellous, or fraudulent
  - Obscene, offensive, or hateful
  - Threatening, harassing, or abusive
  - Invasive of another's privacy
  - Harmful to minors
- Upload malware, viruses, or other malicious code
- Distribute spam or unsolicited communications

### 3.3 System Abuse

- Attempt to gain unauthorised access to the Service or related systems
- Interfere with or disrupt the Service or servers
- Circumvent security measures or access controls
- Use automated tools to access the Service without authorisation
- Overload or attempt to crash the Service
- Probe, scan, or test vulnerabilities without authorisation
- Reverse engineer, decompile, or attempt to derive source code

### 3.4 Account Misuse

- Share account credentials with unauthorised parties
- Create accounts using false or misleading information
- Use the Service on behalf of a competitor
- Resell, sublicense, or redistribute access to the Service
- Exceed usage limits applicable to your subscription

### 3.5 Data Misuse

- Collect personal data without appropriate consent or legal basis
- Use personal data for purposes beyond those disclosed to data subjects
- Retain personal data longer than necessary or permitted
- Fail to implement appropriate security measures for personal data
- Transfer personal data in violation of data protection laws
- Upload sensitive personal data beyond what is necessary for property management
